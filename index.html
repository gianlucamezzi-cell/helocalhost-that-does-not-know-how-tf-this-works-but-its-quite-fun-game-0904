About me

I’m an architecture student and designer interested in housing as a right and design that supports comfort, safety, and dignity. My work connects built environments with questions about offline spaces—places where internet access is limited, absent, or intentionally avoided.

Outside architecture, I practice mountaineering: a meditative attention to the present and to making careful steps in complex terrain.
